Challenges And Fixes

What I learned while building and hosting it.

Email delivery after hosting

SMTP required correct Gmail app password, sender settings, From versus To checks, and hosted reset links.

Role-based access

Renter, owner, and admin pages needed correct redirects, permissions, and dashboard behavior.

Booking state management

Requests, active rentals, returns, extensions, and disputes needed clear status rules.

Admin operations

The admin side needed summaries, filters, case review, and enough information to make decisions quickly.

Learning points Debugging made the product stronger
